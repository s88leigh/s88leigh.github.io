About Me

I am passionate about delivering a great product with exceptional business experience to my clients. I collaborate with clients to learn their story, and use innovative design thinking to help design a product that they are excited about.

Before moving to New Hamshire, I studied at Drexel University where I received a BA in Marketing, which I later used to help manage our family business. I subsequently applied it to my own business. With my extensive business experience and artistic background, I have helped clients solve their challenges and deliver exceptional results.

As an advocate of life-long learning and innovative thinking, I continuously seek new inspiration to improve with an open mindset.
